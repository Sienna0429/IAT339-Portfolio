157 Moments User Experience Design

Sienna Wu March 2025

Overview

Who is 157 Moments?

A fashion boutique located in Gastown, offering a carefully curated selection of unique items from around the world, ranging from women's clothing to perfumes and lifestyle products. They place great emphasis on the in-store user experience and offline interactions with customers.

Our Goal

Enhance users' in-store experience by redesigning the interaction model between users and the store.

Process

01

Ethnography

Through more in-depth interviews with them, participation in events, and observation of interactions during events, we narrowed down the broad goal of improving user experience into 3 specific concentrations:

  • Product
  • Event
  • Brand Awareness

However, since we didn't get enough data from the customers, we still lacked a deep understanding of the customer experience.

02

Persona

When analysing personas, we found that Both staff and customers wanted more meaningful interactions with each other. Though, not way too much. Since the current event process is not well organised, some consumers may feel confused and bored during the event. The lack of sufficient product information is indeed one of the key factors in the degradation of the customer experience.
